About Us

Our Crusade Against Alcohol

We wanted to provide an establishment with multiple options of entertainment for the people out there that don't drink alcohol or are struggling with alcohol abuse.
Instead of having your gameday centered around booze, bad food & dangerously rowdy groups; you can come to Sprouts & have an amazing time with your
friends/like minded people and most importantly feel great at the end of the night (& the next morning ).

Our Love For Fitness

Since we were already looking out for your waistline by serving only healthy meals & beverages we decided to take health to the next level for our fitness lovers.
Just in case that wasn't enough we decided to add an indoor gym with showers & locker rooms.


Ocean View With Things To Do

We always envisioned that our rooftop patio would be overlooking the ocean. So we built it! Again, we couldn't stop there so we added rooftop bars, hot tubs, a yoga area,
an outdoor lifting/calesthenics area, a pool, and even miniature golf! .

Our Man Cave Rentals

Some people only want to watch the big game with close friends and family; but for various reasons they don't want to host or can't host the event at their house.
We wanted solve for that issue as well by creating several themed rooms that any group of friends can rent out by the hour.
